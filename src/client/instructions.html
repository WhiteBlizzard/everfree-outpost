How to Play

  • Use ← ↓ ↑ → to walk. Hold Shift while walking to run.
  • Use A to interact with objects in the world, such as trees, chests, and anvils. The effect depends on the object. For example, interacting with a chest will open it. Most solid objects can be interacted with in some way.
  • Use E to open your inventory. Use ↓ ↑ to select an item, and Space to close the inventory window. The currently selected item is shown in the top left corner of the screen.
  • Use D to use the currently selected item. Some items can be used on objects. For example, you can use an axe on a tree to cut down the tree.
  • Menus and dialogs (including this one) can be closed by pressing Space.
